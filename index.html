About Us

  • Goodly Heritage Initiative is a Nigerian non governmental organization (NGO) which focuses mostly on the health sector of the economy. It is founded by Ms Chimezie Udochi Anyiam, a seasoned Nigerian Enterpreneur.
  • The main aim and objective of the organization is the health betterment of the citizens of Nigeria. We are into health advocacy especially in the area of Diabetes ailment.
  • We educate the public on health issues, do free screening of blood sugar levels and also run BP checks. We provide drugs if necessary.
  • In the education sector, we educate the students on topics like gender equality, girl child education and provide educational resources on those topics.

    Mission

    Our aim is a healthier population, supported by quality health care that can be equitably accessed. We sensitize the public on various health topics which helps to raise health literacy among the general public.

    Vision

    To promote health and wellness, to preserve reproductive services and to advocate for quality healthcare through sensitization and education.
